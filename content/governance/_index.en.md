Träwelling is a project run by a small group of volunteers in their spare time, funded entirely out of their own pockets until now.
As of May 2026, we are a registered association based in Karlsruhe, which unites people who are committed to the project. You can find out more about the association and the background to its formation at https://traewelling.org/
